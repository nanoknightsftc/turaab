Troubleshooting Approach

My Diagnostic Process

  • Gather Information: Understanding the problem symptoms and when they occur
  • Isolate Variables: Testing components individually to narrow down the issue
  • Check the Basics: Verifying connections, power, and obvious physical issues
  • Systematic Testing: Using process of elimination to identify faulty components
  • Verify the Fix: Stress testing and monitoring to ensure the problem is resolved
